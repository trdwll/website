<h2>New House</h2>
<p>So last month on July 4th, 2020 I moved into a new house. The reason behind this is that our landlords had decided to sell the house that we lived in since I was a kid. (~14 years we lived there) No worries, we moved out before the place sold so we wouldn't have to worry about it later. In fact, our new house is quite nice.</p>
